    About Department of Computer Science and Engineering

    The Department of Computer Science & Engineering at AOT University, established in July 2003 under the leadership of Prof. Prosenjit Das, offers a dynamic 4-year B.Tech programme focusing on data science, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. Our department boasts modern facilities including dedicated class and tutorial rooms, seminar spaces, faculty rooms, and specialized laboratories equipped with high-performance HP computers. We provide hands-on learning experiences in areas such as Data Structures, Computer Architecture, Google Innovation, Network & Cyber Security, and System Engineering. Our infrastructure includes Sun Ultrasparc IIIi and IBM X-Series servers, complemented by licensed software like Oracle, MATLAB, Java JDK, and Adobe Creative Suite. For inquiries and collaborations, please contact us at hod_cse@aot.edu.in.
