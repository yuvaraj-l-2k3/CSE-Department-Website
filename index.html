    | ABOUT US

    The department, established in 1996, offers a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Initially, the annual intake was 60. In response to the growing global demand, the intake was increased to 120 from 2003 onwards. Since its inception, the department has consistently pursued excellence in Computer Science Engineering. The goal is to produce graduates who are technocrats and entrepreneurs to meet the nation's computing demands. Additionally, it promotes active industry-institute collaboration by identifying areas of interest and participating in sponsored research projects and consultancy services. The department is dedicated to staying at the forefront of advancements in the field.
